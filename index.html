    Football Analytics with Python

    Python-based football analytics, from raw Statsbomb event data to tactical analyses, physical performance metrics, and deep dives into the game's most interesting questions.

    Series

    Series 1 — Getting Started

    The foundations. Load Statsbomb data, draw a pitch, build shot maps, pass networks, and heatmaps. No prior football analytics knowledge needed.

    Start with 1.1 — The Data

    Series 2 — Tactical Analysis

    xG models, pressing metrics, through balls, freeze frames, carries, and 360° data. How to measure what teams actually do on the pitch.

    Start with 2.1 — What Is xG?

    Series 3 — Physical Performance

    GPS tracking data in professional football. What the metrics mean, how positions differ, and how to build your own analysis pipeline.

    Start with P.1 — GPS Introduction

    Series 4 — Deep Dives

    Messi's career in La Liga. Barcelona 2015/16. Women's World Cup 2019 vs. 2023. Champions League finals. Leverkusen's unbeaten season. Long-form analyses with a clear thesis.

    Start with 4.1 — Messi Career

    Data

    Statsbomb Open Data: 3,464 matches, 75 seasons, event data + 360° tracking. Free and open for research and education.

    Synthetic GPS Dataset: Physical performance data generated from peer-reviewed sports science benchmarks (Mohr et al. 2003, Bradley et al. 2009, Di Salvo et al. 2007).
